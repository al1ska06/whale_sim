Nigenda-Morales, S.F., Lin, M., Nuñez-Valencia, P.G. et al. The genomic footprint of whaling and isolation in fin whale populations. Nat Commun 14, 5465 (2023). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-023-40052-z



"P1": Tracking P(5,000) recovery over 20 generations with varying bottleneck sizes (10-2000)

"P2": Tracking P(5,000) recovery over 20 generations with bottleneck (50, 305, 500) over mutation selection coefficients (10^-5 - .1), defined on SLiM here(https://benhaller.com/slim/SLiM_Manual.pdf). 

"P3": Tracking P(5,000) recovery over (2, 12... 92) generations with bottleneck size 305.
